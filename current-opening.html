ASSISTANT MANAGER-ACCOUNTING & BUSINESS SUPPORT

Designation Assistant Manager
Experience 2-3 years
Division Accounting and Business Support
Location Varanasi
Number of Openings 1-2
Closing Date 20th Jan 2024

Job Description

Ensuring regulatory compliances of various clients on a regular basis
Verification and advisory on records, documents, registers etc. as required by law
Preparation of replies to show cause notices and other proceedings before the authorities
and representations in person
Research on applicable case laws, articles, write-ups to substantiate tax positions
Preparation of advisory and expert opinions
Timely raising of invoices on client and collection as per defined targets
Regular visits on client place to give comfort to client on deliverables
Responding and providing solutions on technical issues to clients on time
Preparation of weekly To-do of the team and discussion with Manager/Partner
Timely escalation of any important matter of clients with seniors
Ensuring systems and processes are being followed for the team
Building performance score cards at the beginning of each year for self and ensuring meeting
targets as per score card
Knowledge dissemination to team members on technical topics through presentations and discussions
Writing articles on professional topics

Desired Candidate Profile

Motivated and ability to provide solutions
Average communication skills to provide a comfort to clients
Should have experience of working with both Indian and foreign clients
Fully conversant with English language – read, write and spoken
Qualified CA, with 1-2 years of post-qualification experience in professional services firm
or more than 5 years post-qualification experience in industry.
Experience on litigation support with representations before the authorities in dealing with
GST audits, show cause notices, assessment proceedings, appellate proceedings and drafting
or replies including research on applicable case laws.
Working knowledge of related areas including accounting, accounting standards, payroll
processing, direct taxes (upto filing of tax return), FEMA, RBI regulations and Corporate
laws
Good practical exposure of GST. Working knowledge of Customs.
