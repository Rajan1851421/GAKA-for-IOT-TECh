Our Network

Our strength lies in our strong network of 8 regional offices and 6 international desks which are supported by our associations and affiliations. Accreditations, recognitions and Trade Chamber memberships enhance this network.

REGIONAL OFFICES

With our head office in Gurgaon and director led offices in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kochi, Mumbai and New Delhi our PAN India presence ensures that our local expertise benefits our clients.

JOINT VENTURE

SCS Global is an international group of advisory/consulting firms and independent accounting firms established to provide multinational clients with support to start up and manage operations in Asian countries.

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS DESKS

Our International Business Desks have been set up with the help of our associates in many countries around the world. In a similar manner, we also have dedicated India desks with our director offices.

The International Desk facilitates and guides international companies in their India investment plans whilst also assisting Indian companies doing business in countries like Japan, Switzerland, Australia, Korea, USA, Germany, France, and UK.
